About Sobha One World

Redefining community living through sophisticated design and vast green expanses.

Sobha One World is an landmark integrated township taking shape across a spectacular 300-acre landscape in the high-growth residential corridor of Greater Whitefield, East Bengaluru. Developed by the renowned Sobha Limited, this mega residential enclave represents a flawless consolidation of expansive scale, architectural innovation, and forward-looking urban design.

The initial release covers an area of 48 acres, introducing 18 architectural towers that ascend to a height of 54 floors, making them dominant features in the East Bangalore skyline. This phase is projected to include around 5,400 residences, offering diverse options ranging from modern 2 BHK layouts to sprawling 4 BHK homes. At the heart of the community stands a lavish 75,000 sq ft clubhouse, surrounded by over 55 bespoke amenities. Embracing nature-focused living, roughly two-thirds of the layout is reserved for scenic landscaping, open spaces, and theme parks.

Positioned on the evolving Hoskote-Whitefield corridor, the project has excellent proximity to critical business hubs such as ITPL, Whitefield, KR Puram, and the Outer Ring Road. Residents will benefit from immediate access to the upcoming Satellite Town Ring Road (STRR) and the Bangalore-Chennai Expressway, enabling short commutes to the Kempegowda International Airport while offering a peaceful retreat away from the congested city center.

Each apartment at Sobha One World is designed with a core focus on spatial efficiency, natural ventilation, and daylighting. The height of the towers ensures residents enjoy sweeping, uninterrupted views of the cityscape. Supported by Sobha's unmatched construction standards and an upcoming in-house commercial retail zone, all lifestyle necessities and leisure activities are integrated directly within your neighborhood.

Smart Integrated Township Layout

The 300-acre master plan is optimized for walking, cycling, and zero-pollution transit corridors. With towers occupying only a minor footprint, the remaining land features lagoons, lush themed parks, sports zones, and tree-lined boulevards.

  • Wide internal roads with bicycle lanes.
  • Rainwater harvesting systems and solid waste management.
  • Integrated commercial complex with grocery stores and pharmacy.

Structure & Walls

Precast concrete shear wall structural system. RCC framework engineered for seismic zone compliance and supreme acoustic isolation.

Premium Flooring

Double charged vitrified tiles in living room, dining, and bedrooms. Anti-skid ceramic tiling in toilets and wide balcony decks.

Electrical & Automation

Fire-resistant copper cabling with modular switches. Automation pre-wiring for smart switches, main-door video locks, and high-speed fiber internet.

Bath Fittings

Premium sanitary ware and CP fittings (Grohe/Kohler or equivalent). Concealed cisterns and glass partition shower stalls.

Smart Space Utilization

Residences are tailored with deep balconies and zero-dead-space floor configurations. High floor-to-ceiling clearances enhance natural light capture.
